# Peer Companies of ValueAdd Research and Analytics Solutions LLP

ValueAdd Research and Analytics Solutions LLP is a Thane/Mumbai-based (with a New York office) research and analytics solutions provider founded in 2015. It offers capital-markets research (investment banking research, equity research, fixed income & credit research) and business strategy/consulting research (industry analysis, market research, competitor benchmarking, M&A research, due diligence, primary surveys), and is increasingly pivoting toward a "Research + AI" model (https://in.linkedin.com/company/valueadd-research-&-analytics-solutions; https://www.greatcompanies.in/post/girish-bhise-founder-and-ceo-at-valueadd-research-and-analytics-solutions-llp). It operates in the Knowledge Process Outsourcing (KPO) / financial research outsourcing space (https://www.ambitionbox.com/overview/valueadd-research-and-analytics-solutions-overview).

Below is a consolidated list of peer/competitor companies, grouped by how the evidence identifies them.

## 1. Peers Named by LinkedIn ("Similar Companies")
LinkedIn's "Similar Companies" listing for ValueAdd (https://in.linkedin.com/company/valueadd-research-&-analytics-solutions) includes:

- **Tirthan Capital** – Investment Management (Pune, Maharashtra)
- **Systematix Group** – Financial Services (Mumbai, Maharashtra)
- **Equirus** – Financial Services (Mumbai, Maharashtra)
- **Rezolve AddVenture – Pune** – IT Services and IT Consulting (Pune)
- **Dalal Street Investment Journal** – Investment Advice (Pune)
- **Ambit Private Limited** – Financial Services (Mumbai)
- **Purnartha PMS** – Financial Services (Pune)
- **Atrivo** – IT Services and IT Consulting (Sweden)
- **Axience** – Business Consulting and Services (Mumbai)
- **Dr. Mylissa's Medical Boutique** – Medical Practices (Florida) — *note: this appears to be an unrelated/algorithmic mismatch and is not a genuine peer.*

*Caveat: LinkedIn's "Similar Companies" list mixes brokerages/asset managers with consulting firms and even unrelated entities, so it is a loose proxy rather than a curated competitor set.*

## 2. Competitors Named by YNOS
The startup database YNOS (https://www.ynos.in/startup/valueadd-research-and-analytics-solutions-315855) lists "possible competitors" as:

- **Beehive Capital** (2012)
- **Brandessence Market Research and Consulting** (2017)
- **Basic Roots** (2017)
- Plus 7 others (not enumerated in the evidence)

## 3. Closely Comparable Research/Analytics Outsourcing Peers
Beyond the directory listings, the evidence points to firms operating in the same boutique financial-research / KPO niche as ValueAdd:

- **EliteAge Research & Analytics** – Delhi-based boutique business & financial research firm offering strategic research, valuation, and risk management to global clients in private equity and investment banking; explicitly a peer of the same size band (11–50 employees) (https://ensun.io/search/financial-research/india).
- **TresVista Financial Services** – Global financial-sector research and investment services provider (financial modeling, industry research, portfolio management) (https://ensun.io/search/financial-research/india).
- **CRISIL (CRISIL Global Research & Risk Solutions / S&P Global)** – High-end financial research, market intelligence, and risk analytics (https://ensun.io/search/financial-research/india).
- **White Capers** – India-based outsourcing firm offering investment research, equity research, financial modeling, valuations, due diligence, and advisory — a direct service overlap with ValueAdd (http://www.whitecapers.com/financial-research-and-analysis.php).
- **SmartAnalyst** – KPO provider in the financial-research outsourcing space serving the buy-side (https://www.integrity-research.com/trading-labor-on-wall-street-examining-india%E2%80%99s-kpo-space/).
- **ICRA Analytics** – Financial research, fixed income/bond valuation, mutual fund research, and knowledge services (https://www.icraanalytics.com/).
- **SG Analytics** – Market research and business insights provider, including primary/secondary and strategy-consulting research (https://www.sganalytics.com/blog/market-research-companies-in-india/).

A direct comparison was also made (by Bounce Watch) between ValueAdd and **International Private Banking Systems**, though the two differ substantially in focus (wealth-management software vs. research outsourcing) (https://bouncewatch.com/compare/international-private-banking-systems/valueadd-research-and-analytics-solutions-llp).

## 4. Larger KPO / Financial-Research Outsourcing Players (Broader Industry Peers)
ValueAdd sits within the broader KPO and data-analytics-outsourcing industry, where larger competitors include firms repeatedly cited as leaders in financial research/analytics outsourcing:

- **WNS** (Mumbai-based KPO in analytics, finance, risk) (https://www.timedoctor.com/blog/kpo-companies/)
- **Boston Analytics** (investment research for investment banks, sovereign funds, PE firms) (https://www.timedoctor.com/blog/kpo-companies/)
- **EXL (EXL Service Holdings)**, **Genpact**, **Mu Sigma**, **Fractal Analytics**, **Tiger Analytics**, **LatentView Analytics**, and the large consulting/IT firms (Accenture, TCS, Wipro, Capgemini, Cognizant, IBM, Deloitte) (https://www.mordorintelligence.com/industry-reports/data-analytics-outsourcing-market/companies; https://www.quora.com/What-are-the-top-10-analytics-firms-in-India)

*Caveat: These are scaled enterprises far larger than ValueAdd (which has 11–50 employees) and are "peers" only in the sense of operating in the same broad industry, not as head-to-head boutique competitors.*

---

## Summary of Most Relevant Peers
For a boutique financial-research outsourcing LLP like ValueAdd, the **most directly comparable peers** are: **EliteAge Research & Analytics, TresVista, White Capers, SmartAnalyst, Axience, and Brandessence Market Research & Consulting**, with **CRISIL/S&P Global Research, ICRA Analytics, and SG Analytics** as larger, more established competitors in overlapping research domains. Directory-driven lists (LinkedIn, YNOS) add financial-services and consulting names (Ambit, Equirus, Systematix, Beehive Capital, etc.), though some of these are brokerages/asset managers rather than research-outsourcing competitors, and at least one LinkedIn match (Dr. Mylissa's Medical Boutique) appears to be a data artifact.
